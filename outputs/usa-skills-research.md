## VISA / IMMIGRATION PATHWAYS

### H-1B Visa (Specialty Occupations)
- **Purpose:** For foreign professionals in specialty occupations requiring at least a bachelor's degree
- **Annual Cap:** 65,000 regular + 20,000 for U.S. master's degree holders = 85,000 total
- **Duration:** Initially 3 years, extendable to 6 years; further extensions possible with pending green card application
- **2026 Updates:** A weighted selection process favoring higher-skilled and higher-paid workers took effect February 27, 2026. An additional $100,000 fee applies to certain initial H-1B petitions filed on or after September 21, 2025
- **FY2027 Registration:** Opens March 4 through March 19, 2026
- **Prevailing Wage:** Employers must pay the higher of prevailing wage or actual wage for similar employees
- **Top Sectors:** Technology, Finance, Healthcare, Engineering, Consulting

### L-1 Visa (Intracompany Transfer)
- **Purpose:** For multinational employees transferring to a U.S. office
- **L-1A (Executives/Managers):** Up to 7 years
- **L-1B (Specialized Knowledge):** Up to 5 years
- **No Annual Cap:** Unlike H-1B, L-1 has no numerical limit
- **Best For:** Global companies moving key talent to U.S. operations

### O-1 Visa (Extraordinary Ability)
- **Purpose:** For individuals with extraordinary ability or achievement in sciences, business, arts, education, or athletics
- **No Annual Cap:** No lottery or numerical limit
- **Duration:** Up to 3 years initially, with 1-year extensions
- **Requirements:** Evidence of sustained national or international acclaim (awards, published work, patents, high compensation)
- **Best For:** Top-tier AI researchers, security experts, leading engineers, renowned designers

### EB Employment-Based Green Cards
- **EB-1:** Priority workers (extraordinary ability, outstanding professors, multinational executives)
- **EB-2:** Advanced degree professionals or those with exceptional ability; includes EB-2 NIW (National Interest Waiver) for STEM professionals
- **EB-3:** Skilled workers (minimum 2 years training/experience), professionals (bachelor's degree), other workers
- **Per-Country Limits:** Wait times vary significantly by nationality, with India and China facing multi-year backlogs

### STEM OPT Extension
- **Purpose:** Extends Optional Practical Training for F-1 graduates with STEM degrees
- **Duration:** 24 months beyond initial 12-month OPT (36 months total work authorization)
- **Requirements:** STEM degree on DHS Designated Degree Program List, employment with E-Verify registered employer, Form I-983 training plan
- **Filing Fee:** $410, processing takes 3-5 months
- **Maximum Unemployment:** 150 days cumulative during entire OPT + STEM OPT period

### TN Visa (USMCA/NAFTA Professionals)
- **Purpose:** For Canadian and Mexican professionals in designated occupations
- **Duration:** Up to 3 years, renewable indefinitely
- **No Cap:** No annual numerical limit
- **Eligible Occupations:** Engineers, accountants, scientists, computer systems analysts, and other professional roles
